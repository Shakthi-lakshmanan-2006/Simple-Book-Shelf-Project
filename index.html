Book-Shelf

To Kill a Mockingbird

Harper Lee

Set in the racially tense American South, this novel follows young Scout Finch. Her father, a lawyer, defends an innocent Black man wrongly accused of a crime. A timeless story of justice, innocence, and growing up.

The Alchemist

Paulo Coelho

A young shepherd named Santiago follows his dream to find hidden treasure in Egypt. Along the way, he discovers deeper truths about life, love, and destiny. A beautiful tale of self-discovery and purpose.

Pride and Prejudice

Jane Austen

Elizabeth Bennet navigates love, pride, and social expectations. She clashes and connects with the reserved Mr. Darcy. A witty, romantic look at 19th-century English society.

Atomic Habits

James Clear

Learn how tiny changes can lead to remarkable results. This book breaks down how habits are formed and sustained. A practical guide for building better daily routines.
